# 🩺 PulsePoint AI

> **Predict. Prevent. Protect.**

PulsePoint AI is an AI-powered healthcare companion designed to transform healthcare from **reactive treatment** to **proactive prevention**. It brings the entire healthcare journey into a single platform—helping users understand symptoms, assess chronic disease risks, manage medical records, receive medicine reminders, locate nearby healthcare facilities, and respond instantly during emergencies.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Healthcare today is fragmented and reactive.

People often rely on internet searches for symptoms, struggle to understand medical reports, forget medications, lose prescriptions, and switch between multiple applications for consultations, diagnostics, pharmacies, and emergency services.

PulsePoint AI solves this by providing an intelligent, all-in-one healthcare ecosystem powered by AI.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

PulsePoint AI combines artificial intelligence with modern healthcare services to deliver personalized guidance, early disease prediction, secure medical record management, emergency assistance, and healthcare accessibility—all from one platform.
